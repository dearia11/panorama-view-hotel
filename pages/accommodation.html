ACCOMMODATIONS

Presidential Suite

Features :
  • Each suite offers 170 sqm / 1,830 sqf of luxury
  • Sydney Harbour, Sydney Harbour Bridge, and Sydney Opera House views.
  • Separate sitting room, kitchenette and dining room.
  • Marble bathroom with separate shower cubicle, large soaking tub and plush Panoramic View bathrobes and towels.
  • Access to the exclusive Horizon Club Lounge with its full range of privileges.

Executive Room

Features :
  • Each room offers 45 – 50 sqm / 484 – 538 sqf of luxury.
  • Panoramic views of Sydney Opera House, Sydney Harbour Bridge and Sydney Harbour.
  • Separate sitting room and kitchenette.
  • Marble bathroom contains a separate bath and shower, plush Panoramic View towels and bathrobes.

Deluxe Room

Features :
  • Each room offers 40 – 50 sqm / 430 – 538 sqf of luxury.
  • Partial views of Sydney Opera House and Circular Quay and CBD.  
  • Marble bathroom contains a separate bath and shower, plush Panoramic View towels and bathrobes.
  • Accommodation rooms can accommodate a maximum of 2 adults and 2 children (under 12 years of age) in existing bedding and 1 rollaway bed (additional charge applies).

Amenities for Each Room

  • Bath & Personal Care
  • Media & Entertainment
  • Office Equipment & Stationery
  • Refreshment
